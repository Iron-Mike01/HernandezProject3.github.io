Trumps ICE CREAM Parlor





Welcome To Trump Ice Cream Parlor Open Daily: 11am – 8pm Located in the Atrium of NYC’s iconic Trump Tower on Fifth Avenue, Trump’s Ice Cream Parlor offers over 24 flavors of ice cream and sorbet each day. Just like the classic ice cream parlors of old, Trump’s Ice Cream Parlor had all of the classic favorites from thick shakes and banana splits to root beer floats and over the top sundaes. Also try our delicious hand-made crepes, fresh juices, fruit smoothies and low-fat frozen yogurt.
